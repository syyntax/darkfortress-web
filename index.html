Who We Are

Dark Fortress Security is a San Antonio, Texas-based cybersecurity company founded by an Air Force veteran with 20 years of active duty service. With over a decade of experience in cybersecurity, we specialize in network penetration testing and consulting to help organizations fortify their digital infrastructure. Our mission is to provide cutting-edge solutions that empower businesses and individuals to operate with confidence in an increasingly digital world.

Our unique combination of military discipline, technical expertise, and a commitment to excellence sets us apart. We are proud to serve as your trusted partner in defending against cyber threats and building unassailable digital strongholds.
